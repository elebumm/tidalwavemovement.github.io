WHO WE ARE

We lost our friend Mat to mental health in november of this year. Mat who died at 34 lived a selfless life of love, compassion and kindness. In Memory of him we have started the Tidal Wave Movement to initiate a campaign of love from coast to coast across the Canada. Matt was known for recording locals artists at his studio “poorboy records” often at no cost. He would record a live performance video and leave you with a Poorboy T-shirt when you were done. So we think it makes perfect sense to tribute him though the mediums of music, film and fashion. We’re very excited for you to see what’s to come, for now subscribe to our newsletter below to stay in the loop! Love like a Tidal wave!
